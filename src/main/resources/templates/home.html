The Frontend Integration Zoo

A hands-on lab of enterprise frontend integration patterns — the kind that were a "temporary" shortcut in 2014 and are load-bearing today. Each exhibit breaks on purpose, then shows a safer alternative.

Two real origins, one process. The portal shell is served from localhost:8080 and the embedded "legacy" apps from localhost:8081. Your browser treats those as different origins, so the cross-origin pain in these exhibits is real — not simulated.
